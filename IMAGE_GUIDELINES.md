IMAGE REQUIREMENTS

Use real industrial photography whenever possible.

Preferred sources:

- Unsplash
- Pexels
- Industrial stock libraries
- Licensed project imagery

Avoid AI-generated images whenever possible.

If AI images are used:

- must appear realistic
- must show correct PPE
- must show realistic welding operations
- must show realistic fabrication environments
- must not contain distorted hands, tools or equipment

Recommended image placement:

- Hero backgrounds: high-quality realistic industrial scenes (offshore, heavy fabrication, construction)
- Section backgrounds: contextual project imagery matching the section topic (pipelines, refineries, data centers)
- Industry cards: representative, focused photography with clear subject and correct PPE
- Project case images: licensed project imagery where available

Follow these rules for any image additions or replacements. When in doubt, prefer licensed project photography or trusted industrial stock over AI-generated content.
